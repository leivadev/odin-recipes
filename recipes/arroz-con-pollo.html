Arroz con pollo (panamanian style)

Description:

This repice is very emblematic and so popular for panamanians, we eat this food at lunch, especially in parties, birthdays, or in special days like new year or christmas. Arroz con pollo (rice with chicken in english) is traditionally a dish with cooked and soft yellow rice, combined with vegetables and little chunks of chicken.

This recipe will take you about 1hr - 1hr 30 minutes to finish it and its enough for 6 portions with this recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2kg (4.4 lb) of boneless and skinless chicken.
  • 1kg (2.2 lb) of white rice.
  • 4 aj√≠ dulce (sweet chilli pepper).
  • 4 tomatoes.
  • 4 bell peppers (2 greens and 2 reds).
  • 1 large white onion.
  • 4 garic gloves.
  • 2 tablespoons of oil (i will use olive oil for this recipe).
  • 1 celery stem.
  • 1/2 cup of green peas and carrots.
  • 1/2 tablespoon of fresh chopped cilantro.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of annato and cumin powder.

Steps:

  1. Add 5 cups of water, the chicken, tomato, annato, cumin, salt and pepper in a large pot and boil over medium heat for 25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked.
  2. Pick the chicken and shred it in thick or slim strips. Important: Keep the chicken stock for later.
  3. In another pot add and heat the olive oil and aggregate the onion, garlic, celery and the bell peppers, stir fry it for 3 minutes, then add 2 cups of chcken stock and mix it.
  4. Add the white rice, shredded chicken, half the cilantro, carrots and mit very well to integrate, let it cook till the stock reduces.
  5. Low the heat, cover the rice pot and let it cook for 20 minutes to finish it.
  6. Uncover, add the rest of the cilantro and mix it for the last time.
  7. Enjoy it!
